About Me

I discovered my passion for programming as a high school freshman through the Junior Knights program, hosted by the UCF Department of Computer Science, where I learned Python and competitive programming. This experience inspired me to explore computer science further by taking high school electives, building platformer games in Scratch, and learning JavaScript. Alongside academics, I launched a reselling business that generated over half a million dollars in revenue by utilizing APIs, proxies, and custom checkout bots, deepening my interest in technology and solidifying my decision to pursue a Computer Science degree.

Now studying at the University of Central Florida, I have gained proficiency in Java and C while completing coursework in Object Oriented Software Development, Object-Oriented Programming, Systems Software, and more. These experiences have honed my technical and problem-solving skills. I am passionate about developing innovative solutions to real-world challenges through technology and am dedicated to applying my skills to impactful software engineering and development projects.
